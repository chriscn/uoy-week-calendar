University of York's Week Numbers

This page provides an iCalendar (RFC 2445) format of the University of York's week numbers, according to the term dates.

It is compatible with most calendar software, the program provides three different calendars depending on your circumstance. Simply adding the following URL to your Calendar software:

  • Undergraduate - https://localhost:9000/calendar?type=ug
  • Postgraduate - https://localhost:9000/calendar?type=pg
  • Staff - https://localhost:9000/calendar?type=staff

The program should support up to the academic year 2029/2030; and is easily updatable past that.
